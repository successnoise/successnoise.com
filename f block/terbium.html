Terbium

Terbium is a chemical element with the symbol Tb and atomic number 65. It is a silvery-white, rare earth metal that is malleable, and ductile. The ninth member of the lanthanide series, terbium is a fairly electropositive metal that reacts with water, evolving hydrogen gas.


Interesting Facts :

→ Terbium does not tarnish rapidly in air.
→ It is soft enough to be cut with a knife.
