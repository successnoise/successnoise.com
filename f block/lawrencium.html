Lawrencium

Lawrencium is a synthetic chemical element with the symbol Lr and atomic number 103. It is named in honor of Ernest Lawrence, inventor of the cyclotron, a device that was used to discover many artificial radioactive elements.


Interesting Facts :

→ Currently, there are no real uses for lawrencium, other than in research.
→ Lawrencium is named after Ernest O. Lawrence, who created the cyclotron in 1934.
