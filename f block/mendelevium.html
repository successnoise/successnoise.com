Mendelevium

Mendelevium is a synthetic element with the symbol Md and atomic number 101. A metallic radioactive transuranium element in the actinide series, it is the first element by atomic number that currently cannot be produced in macroscopic quantities through neutron bombardment of lighter elements.


Interesting Facts :

→ Mendelevium has no known biological role.
→ Mendelevium is a radioactive rare earth metal.
