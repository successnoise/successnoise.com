Bismuth

Bismuth is a chemical element with the symbol Bi and atomic number 83. It is a post-transition metal, radioactive, and one of the pnictogens with chemical properties resembling its lighter group 15 siblings arsenic and antimony.


Interesting Facts :

→ Bismuth was among the first 10 metals to be discoved and classified.
→ Egyptians used bismuth in their cosmetic products.
