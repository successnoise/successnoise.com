Caesium

Caesium is a chemical element with the symbol Cs and atomic number 55. It is a soft, silvery-golden alkali metal with a melting point of 28.5 °C, which makes it one of only five elemental metals that are liquid at or near room temperature. Its symbol is Cs.


Interesting Facts :

→ Cesium is incredibly accurate at timekeeping and is used in atomic clocks.
→ It is the most electropoisitive and nost alkaline element.
