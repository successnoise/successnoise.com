Magnesium

Magnesium is a chemical element with the symbol Mg and atomic number 12. It is a shiny gray solid which shares many physical and chemical properties with the other five alkaline earth metals. It is an essential element for the survival of both plants and animals.


Interesting Facts :

→ Photosynthesis cannot be carried out in plants without Mg.
→ It is involved in 300+ chemical reactions in our body.
