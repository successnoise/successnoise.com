Helium

Helium is a chemical element with the chemical symbol He and Atoomic Number 2. It is colourless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic, inert, monoatomic gas and the first in the noble gas group in the periodic table.


Interesting Facts :

→ Its melting and boiling point are the lowest among all elements.
→ It is the second lightest element with an atomic mass of 4u.
