Sodium

Sodium (Na), is the 11th chemical element of the periodic table. Sodium is a very soft silvery-white metal. Sodium is the most common alkali metal and the sixth most abundant element on Earth, comprising 2.8 percent of Earth's crust.


Interesting Facts :

→ Sodium is highly reactive.
→ The transfer of nerve impulse between axons is done by Sodium.
