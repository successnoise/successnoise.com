Chromium

Chromium is a chemical element with the symbol Cr and atomic number 24. It is the first element in group 6. It is a steely-grey, lustrous, hard, and brittle transition metal. Chromium metal is valued for its high corrosion resistance and hardness.


Interesting Facts :

→ It is used as a plating material in cars which gives a glossy finish.
→ It doesn't rust.
