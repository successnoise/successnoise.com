Dubnium

Dubnium is a synthetic chemical element with the symbol Db and atomic number 105. It is highly radioactive: the most stable known isotope, dubnium-268, has a half-life of about 28 hours. This greatly limits extended research on the element. Dubnium does not occur naturally on Earth and is produced artificially.


Interesting Facts :

→ It is named after the Russian town Dubna where it was first made.
→ All of its isotopes are radioactive in nature.
